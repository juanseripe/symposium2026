# Call for Papers  
### PhD Annual Symposium 2026

---

## Scope and Topics

The PhD Annual Symposium welcomes research contributions from doctoral students across all areas of business and management, including (but not limited to):

- Operations and Supply Chain Management  
- Finance and Accounting  
- Marketing  
- Strategy  
- Information Systems  
- Organizational Behavior and Human Resources  
- Economics and Public Policy  

Both theoretical and applied research are encouraged.

---

## Submission Guidelines

- Submissions must represent **ongoing or completed doctoral research**
- Papers may be:
  - Full research papers  
  - Extended abstracts  
- All submissions will be reviewed by an academic committee

Detailed submission instructions will be announced shortly.
