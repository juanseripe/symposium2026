---

The detailed program will be published closer to the event.

The Symposium will feature three complementary formats:

- **Oral presentations** (full papers)  
- **Roundtables** (extended abstracts)  
- **Workshops** (interactive sessions)

In addition, the program will include opening and closing remarks, as well as opportunities for networking and informal exchanges.

---

### Preliminary Structure (Indicative)

- **Morning:** Welcome + parallel sessions (oral presentations / roundtables)  
- **Midday:** Lunch + networking  
- **Afternoon:** Workshops + parallel sessions + closing remarks
