### Building entrances

The Hélène-Desmarais Building has several entrances:

- **Carmelle and Rémi-Marcoux Entrance** (main entrance), on Rue De la Gauchetière Ouest — accessible with an automatic door opener button
- **Underground parking entrance**, on Rue De la Gauchetière Ouest — indoor access with an automatic door opener button; 3 reserved spaces near the elevator (P1)
- **Famille Waechter Entrance**, on Beaver Hall Hill, in front of the Square-Victoria–OACI metro access point
- **Famille Karim Entrance**, at the pedestrian passage level from Boulevard René-Lévesque Ouest — door opener button; long outdoor pedestrian walkway

---

### Getting there

- **Metro:** Square-Victoria–OACI — follow the HEC Montréal signs and walk through the corridor to the exit. The Famille Waechter entrance is directly across the street via the pedestrian crossing.
- **Bus:** line 150.
- **Bike:** indoor and outdoor bike racks, plus nearby BIXI stations (see the BIXI app).
- **Car:** parking access on Rue De la Gauchetière Ouest; EV charging stations available.
